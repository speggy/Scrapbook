Guardians fight several different types of enemies in order to keep the citizens of the Last City safe.

  • Fallen
  • Hive
  • Cabal
  • Vex

The Fallen are essentially space pirates that want the Traveler's light all to themselves since it left them some number of years ago. They came to Earth to try to wipe us all out in hopes they could have the Traveler to themselves again.

The Hive are a nasty race. They deal most directly with the darkness that tries to creep into our world.

The Cabal are a giant militarized alien race that kill everything in their path. They too seek to colonize and control every planet in the system.

The Vex are timeline-altering semi-organic robots that travel between timelines and seek universal domination.
