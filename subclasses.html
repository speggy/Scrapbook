Classes Subclasses Enemies

Each Guardian class has 3 subclasses that grant their users different abilities. Each subclass is based on different elements: Solar, Void, and Arc.
Hunters have a solar subclass called Gunslinger, arc subclass called Arcstrider, and void subclass called Nightstalker.
Titans have a solar subclass called Sunbreaker, arc subclass called striker, and void subclass called Sentinal.
Warlocks have a solar subclass called Sunsinger, arc subclass called Stormcaller, and void subclass called Voidwalker.
