This is Project 3 for CS2800, a scrapbook that describes a video game called Destiny. This page serves as my personal field journal for when I was deployed alongside a fellow guardian. Guardians are wielders of the Light and fight to protect the citizens of the Last City on Earth. Here I document various findings such as some of the creatures and foes my partner faced and different kinds of guardians and their abilities.
